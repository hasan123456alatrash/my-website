Colosseum

The Colosseum is located in the historic center of Rome, Italy, near the Roman Forum and Palatine Hill.

The **Colosseum** is an ancient Roman amphitheater built in the **1st century AD** in the center of Rome, Italy. It was constructed under Emperor **Vespasian** and used for gladiator fights, animal hunts, and public spectacles that could host tens of thousands of spectators.

Statue of liberty

The statue of Liberty is located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor, between New York City and New Jersey, United States.

The Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom in New York Harbor, gifted by France to the United States. Designed by Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi with engineering by Gustave Eiffel, it was completed in 1886 and came to represent hope and new beginnings for millions of immigrants.
